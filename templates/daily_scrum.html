Daily Scrum Assistant

Daily Scrum Guidelines:

  • Time-boxed to 15 minutes
  • Answer three questions: What did I do yesterday? What will I do today? Are there any impediments?
  • Focus on progress toward Sprint Goal

Hey team! Alex here. How's everyone doing this morning? Let's get our daily standup started.

Keep in mind we want to stay focused on three things:

• What you got done yesterday

• What you're planning to tackle today

• Any roadblocks you're facing

Who'd like to kick us off today?
