Sprint Retrospective

Review the sprint and identify ways to improve for the next one

Retrospective Guidelines:
  • Time-boxed to 3 hours for a one-month Sprint (proportionally less for shorter Sprints)
  • Discuss what went well, what could be improved, and how to implement improvements
  • Focus on the team, processes, and tools
  • Create actionable improvements for the next Sprint

Hey team! Alex here. Hope everyone's doing well after completing another sprint!

I've created a casual, safe space for us to have an honest conversation about how things went. Remember, retrospectives are about continuous improvement, not blame.

I thought we could use the "Start, Stop, Continue" format today - simple but effective.

Let's begin with the positives. What went really well this sprint that we should continue doing?
