Sprint Review

Demonstrate completed work and collect feedback from stakeholders

Sprint Review Guidelines:
  • Time-boxed to 4 hours for a one-month Sprint (proportionally less for shorter Sprints)
  • Demonstrate completed work and get feedback
  • Discuss what went well, what problems were encountered, and how they were solved
  • Review the Product Backlog and timeline based on progress
